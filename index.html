Method

For Samp:
  1. Wash the samp.
  2. Once you have washed the samp, place in a bowl and leave to soak overnight. *Soak in 1L of cold water.
  3. Cooking samp: Drain soaked samp and place in a pot. Add enough boiling water to cover the samp.
  4. Bring to a boil. Once boiling, reduce heat and simmer for two hours or until soft. Stir every 30 minutes. Add water when necessary.
  5. In a bowl, add powdered milk, cream, mushroom soup, and cheddar sauce with 100 ml of hot water and mix until it forms a paste. Once the paste is formed, add another 150 ml of water. Add to your samp.
  6. Add margarine and Aromat and stir.
  7. Simmer for 20 minutes. Stir occasionally until you get a creamy texture.
For Beef Stew:
  1. Heat oil over medium heat. Brown the beef for 3 ‚Äì 4 minutes on all sides. Set aside.
  2. Sweat the onions and carrots in the same pot, deglaze with wine or stock to scrape off burned beef bits.
  3. Add the beef back into the pot and dissolve stock cubes in 500 g of water then pour over the beef.
  4. Simmer for two hours.
  5. Add potatoes, tomato paste, and Worcestershire sauce.
  6. Simmer for 60 ‚Äì 90 minutes on low heat until tender.
